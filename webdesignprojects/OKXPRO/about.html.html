About Us

Our Profile

Arville Vocational High School was established in 1866 and ratified in 1867 based on the Decree of the Regent of United Kingdom No. 2 of 1867 dated January 27, 1867. Ariville Vocational High School graduated for the first time in 1869 on the Fisheries Agribusiness Expertise Competency. Until 2000 Arville Vocational High School has graduated more than 10000 students.


Arville Vocational High School has professional Human Resources (HR) in Six Expertise Competencies, namely Pharmacy, Culinary, Law, Accountancy, Aviator (Pilot Education), Road and Bridge Design Techniques


Arville Vocational High School present curriculum design integrated with industrial needs where more creations compared theory. So that students can have Portfolio of works, certified, recognized by the world industry, has an entrepreneurial spirit and continuing linear studies.
