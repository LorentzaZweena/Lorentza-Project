Majors We Offer

Here are the majors available at our school

Pharmacy

The Department of Pharmacy is a major that studies everything about medicine. Starting from the chemicals in it, the process of making drugs, the process of packaging drugs, the function and use of drugs, to how to distribute and manage drug stocks.

Culinary

The Department of Food Management is a major that studies food serving techniques by paying attention to aesthetics, quality, taste, and nutritional needs.

Law Major

A law major is a major that studies the sciences that develop in the field of law. The sciences in this field of law develop in practice as well as theory.

Accountancy

The accounting department is a major that is included in the Soshum family of knowledge with a lot of material about calculations. The Accounting Department studies the art of recording, classifying and endeavoring in a certain way in monetary measures.

Aviator (Pilot Education)

Aviation known as air transport, referring to activities surrounding mechanical aviation and the aircraft industry. Aviation in general can be divided into civil aviation and military aviation, both of which have different protocols and aircraft. In short, aviation is everything related to flying an aircraft.

Road and Bridge Design
Techniques

The Department of Road and Bridge Design Engineering is one of the engineering sciences that specializes in planning and making roads and bridges, especially freeways or what we know better as toll roads.
