The implementation of Vocational High Schools is also based on on the provisions in the Law of the Republic of Indonesia No.2 Year 1989 on the national education system chapter IV article 11 paragraph (1) and (3) whose contents include "general types of education, education religious, academic education, and professional education". School vocational secondary by level is equivalent to school upper middle, but the two have different goals". Vocational High School with three grade levels consists of class X, class XI, and class XII. Vocational High School with four levels classes according to the demands of the world of work consist of class X, class XI, class XII and class XIII.


General provisions of article 1 of the Government Regulation of the Republic Indonesia Number 17 of 2010 Article 78 defines that, Schools Vocational Secondary (SMK) as a form of unit formal education that provides vocational education on secondary education level as a continuation of junior high school, MTs, or other equivalent or advanced forms of recognized learning outcomes the same or equivalent of SMP or MTs. SMK can consist of three levels class or four grade levels.


Our program has the following objectives, including the following:

1. To unite the views and ideals of all school residents in realizing the goals of providing education and improving quality.
2. To make it easier to carry out school management tasks according to their respective goals.
3. To make it easier to evaluate school management tasks.
4. To give a real picture of the current state of the school and the year to come.


Our school holds certificate exams every semester. The certificate is a certificate that can allow students to get a job, The Certificate are ISO certificate.

ISO certificate (at least awareness / implementation individual certificate) is a requirement of some companies to ensure that you are familiar with the ongoing business processes and can contribute productively to the company. ISO certificates are issued by certification bodies directly affiliated with ISO organizations. Companies that already have and implement ISO system requirements can apply for certification to the ISO Certification Body.
